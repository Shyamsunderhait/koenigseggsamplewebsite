Regera
The world's faster car ever build

“Regera” translates to “to reign” in Swedish. Wherever it ventures, the Koenigsegg Regera reigns with a combination of a V8 engine and three electric motors. Exceptional performance is just one of its many highlights; the Koenigsegg Regera is unabashedly luxurious and cossets drivers with a level of style and comfort that can only be achieved by a Koenigsegg megacar.

About Us - Koenigsegg

Welcome to Koenigsegg, a pioneering Swedish automotive manufacturer known for producing high-performance hypercars and cutting-edge automotive technology.

Founded in 1994 by Christian von Koenigsegg, our company has become synonymous with innovation, precision engineering, and a relentless pursuit of automotive excellence.

Our Founder - Christian von Koenigsegg

Christian von Koenigsegg, a visionary entrepreneur and automotive enthusiast, established Koenigsegg at the age of 22 with the goal of creating the world's best sports cars.
